Verification Framework

Four Pillars of Fund Integrity

Every transaction verified through FLAME Protocol is validated across four independent dimensions, each producing cryptographic proof.

01

Chain of Title

Complete ownership history from origination to present, cryptographically linked via Merkle tree. Every transfer, encumbrance, and release recorded.

What Banks Verify:
  • Unbroken ownership chain from source
  • All intermediate transfers authenticated
  • No competing claims or encumbrances
  • Constitutional authority of originator
Proof Type: MERKLE_INCLUSION_PROOF
02

Clean Funds Certification

Funds verified against AML/KYC requirements with cryptographic attestation. Source of wealth documented and anchored to compliance frameworks.

What Banks Verify:
  • AML screening completed and passed
  • Source of funds documented
  • No sanctions list matches
  • Compliance attestation signed
Proof Type: COMPLIANCE_ATTESTATION
03

Asset Backing Verification

Physical and digital assets backing the instrument verified through vault attestations, custody proofs, and lien magnitude calculations.

What Banks Verify:
  • Backing instrument identified
  • Custody proof from authorized vault
  • Lien magnitude vs. transaction value
  • No prior encumbrances on collateral
Proof Type: VAULT_ATTESTATION
04

Liquidity Availability

Real-time verification that funds are available for immediate settlement across all 12 rails: ACH, SWIFT, ISO20022, ETH, USDT, USDC, BTC, and more.

What Banks Verify:
  • Funds not locked elsewhere
  • Settlement rail availability confirmed
  • Atomic execution guarantee
  • No pending claims or holds
Proof Type: LIQUIDITY_COMMITMENT
