Verification Framework

Four Pillars of Fund Integrity

Every transaction is validated across four independent dimensions, each producing cryptographic proof verifiable on Ethereum mainnet.

01

Chain of Title

Complete ownership history from origination to present, cryptographically linked via Merkle tree. Every transfer recorded on-chain.

What Banks Verify:
  • Unbroken ownership chain from source
  • All transfers authenticated on mainnet
  • No competing claims or encumbrances
  • Constitutional authority of originator
Proof: MERKLE_INCLUSION
02

Clean Funds Certification

AML/KYC compliance with cryptographic attestation. Source of wealth documented and anchored to immutable compliance records.

What Banks Verify:
  • AML screening completed and passed
  • Source of funds documented on-chain
  • No sanctions list matches
  • Compliance attestation signed
Proof: COMPLIANCE_ATTESTATION
03

Asset Backing Verification

Physical and digital assets verified through vault attestations, custody proofs, and lien magnitude calculations anchored to mainnet.

What Banks Verify:
  • Backing instrument identified on-chain
  • Custody proof from authorized vault
  • Lien magnitude vs. transaction value
  • No prior encumbrances on collateral
Proof: VAULT_ATTESTATION
04

Liquidity Availability

Real-time verification that funds are available for immediate settlement across all rails: ACH, SWIFT, ISO20022, ETH, USDT, USDC, BTC.

What Banks Verify:
  • Funds not locked or committed elsewhere
  • Settlement rail availability confirmed
  • Atomic execution guarantee
  • No pending claims or holds
Proof: LIQUIDITY_COMMITMENT
